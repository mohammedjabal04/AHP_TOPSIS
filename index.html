      During my studies at ENCG Fez — National School of Commerce and Management in Fez, Morocco — I was introduced to powerful decision-making frameworks like AHP and TOPSIS. As I worked through the mathematics behind them, I kept asking myself:
      "Can we automate it — just enter the numbers and let the machine do all the calculations?"

      Other web-based calculators existed, but they were rigid, outdated, or strictly designed for academics. I wanted something entirely different — an elegant, intuitive tool that anyone could use to make a scientific, data-driven decision in any situation. So I built it from scratch.

      Drawing from my hands-on experience in logistics and procurement, and my deep commitment to making quality education accessible, Decision Compass was born. My mission: bridge the gap between rigorous academic theory and practical, everyday choices — and make it completely free, for everyone, forever.
